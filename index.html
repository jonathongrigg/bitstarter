FAQ

What does Lock+Learn do?
Lock+Learn is a replacement lockscreen that you unlock by answering a question. This question is related to the topic of your choice, so you can actively practice every time you need to use your phone!
Awesome! But it isn't secure right?
Correct, although it may function as a password, anyone could Google the answer to gain access to your phone.
